About Us

Paragon Public High School, established in 1970, is a beacon of educational excellence in our community. For over five decades, we have been committed to providing a nurturing and challenging educational environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, personal growth, and social responsibility.

Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that every student receives the support they need to excel academically and personally. We pride ourselves on our ability to adapt to the changing needs of education while maintaining our core values and traditions.

At Paragon, we believe in educating the whole child. Our approach goes beyond academic achievement to include character development, leadership skills, and community engagement. We strive to produce well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of higher education and beyond.

Our Programs

Academic Excellence

Our comprehensive curriculum covers a wide range of subjects, ensuring students receive a well-rounded education. We offer:

  • Advanced Placement (AP) courses in various subjects
  • STEM-focused programs with hands-on learning experiences
  • Language arts and literature studies
  • Social sciences and cultural studies
  • Fine arts and music programs

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in nurturing talents beyond academics. Our school offers:

  • Sports teams in basketball, soccer, swimming, and track & field
  • Debate and public speaking clubs
  • Robotics and coding clubs
  • Environmental and community service groups
  • Drama and theater productions

Our Guiding Principles

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of education, encouraging our students to reach their highest potential in academics, arts, athletics, and personal growth.

Integrity

We foster a culture of honesty, ethics, and accountability, teaching our students the importance of moral character in all their endeavors.

Inclusivity

We celebrate diversity and create an inclusive environment where every student feels valued, respected, and supported.

Innovation

We embrace innovative teaching methods and technologies to prepare our students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Community

We believe in the power of community and encourage active participation in local and global initiatives to develop socially responsible citizens.

Headmaster

Dr. Waqar Abbas Bukhari , the principal of the school, plays a crucial role in shaping the educational environment, acting as both an administrator and a leader. As the head of the institution, Dr. Bukhari is responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations, ensuring that the school adheres to educational standards and policies. This includes managing staff, budgeting, and ensuring that the resources needed for a quality education are available. Dr. Bukhari must also ensure that the school's curriculum meets the needs of the students while promoting a safe and inclusive atmosphere where both academic and personal growth are encouraged. Effective communication with teachers, students, and parents is vital in maintaining a positive and productive school community.

A successful principal like Dr. Waqar Abbas Bukhari not only focuses on the academic progress of students but also plays a key role in fostering their social and emotional development. By supporting extracurricular activities, creating opportunities for leadership, and establishing a culture of respect and discipline, Dr. Bukhari helps students thrive beyond the classroom. The principal is often seen as a mentor and guide, supporting teachers in their professional growth and striving to implement new educational techniques and technologies that improve learning outcomes. Strong leadership, compassion, and an ability to adapt to the ever-changing demands of education are essential traits for Dr. Bukhari to inspire students, faculty, and the wider community.
